Self Balancing Bot
A self-balancing robot works on the principle of an inverse pendulum. By using the motion data from the Digital Motion Processor(DMP) of MPU-6050 which performs the accelerometer-gyroscope sensor fusion, the controller (arduino) gives instructions to the stepper motors so as to reduce instantaneous real-vertical tilt which renders the bot erect. A virtual tilt is introduced in the system-vertical so as to use the balancing action of the bot to maneuver it forwards and backwards. A speed offset is provided in one of the motors to rotate the bot.
